About Me:

I obtained a doctorate from the Discrete Mathematics group at UBC in May 2023. My advisor is Jozsef Solymosi. My research interests include Combinatorics, Discrete Geometry, Number Theory, and Harmonic Analysis. Beginning in fall 2023 I will join the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign as a J.L. Doob Research Assistant Professor.
